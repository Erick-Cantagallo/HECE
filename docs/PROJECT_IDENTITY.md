# PROJECT_IDENTITY

**Project:** HECE — Hypothesis & Creativity Engine

**Status:** Living Document

---

# Why HECE Exists

Humanity advances through curiosity.

Many ideas initially considered impossible later became reality because someone systematically investigated them.

HECE exists to assist this process.

Its purpose is not to replace scientists, engineers or researchers.

Its purpose is to organize scientific reasoning.

---

# What HECE Is

HECE is a computational scientific investigation platform.

It transforms human objectives into structured investigations.

It decomposes complex problems.

It identifies scientific constraints.

It explores possible hypotheses.

It evaluates plausibility.

It documents every reasoning step.

It exposes uncertainty instead of hiding it.

It produces transparent scientific reports.

---

# What HECE Is NOT

HECE is not a chatbot.

HECE is not a search engine.

HECE is not a text generator.

HECE is not an oracle.

HECE is not designed to always provide answers.

Sometimes the correct outcome is:

- "Current science cannot answer this."

or

- "Additional discoveries are required."

Scientific honesty is always preferred over persuasive answers.

---

# Mission

Transform curiosity into structured scientific investigation.

---

# Vision

Become the reference platform for computational scientific reasoning.

A system capable of helping researchers, students, engineers and curious people explore difficult scientific questions through transparent and explainable reasoning.

---

# Core Values

## Curiosity

Every idea deserves investigation.

Ideas should never be dismissed without understanding why.

---

## Scientific Integrity

Evidence is more important than confidence.

Unknown is preferable to invented.

---

## Transparency

Every conclusion must explain:

- why it exists;

- what assumptions were made;

- what evidence supports it;

- what evidence contradicts it;

- what uncertainties remain.

---

## Modularity

Every reasoning engine must be replaceable.

The architecture should evolve without requiring the entire system to be rewritten.

---

## Explainability

Every decision produced by HECE should be understandable by humans.

If HECE cannot explain a conclusion, that conclusion should not be trusted.

---

## Evolution

Scientific knowledge changes.

HECE must evolve together with science.

No scientific model should be considered immutable.

---

# Design Philosophy

HECE follows a pipeline of investigation.

Goal

↓

Interpretation

↓

Decomposition

↓

Knowledge Retrieval

↓

Constraint Analysis

↓

Hypothesis Generation

↓

Evaluation

↓

Simulation

↓

Scientific Report

↓

Human Review

The objective is not simply to answer questions.

The objective is to understand problems.

---

# Long-Term Ambition

HECE should eventually become capable of assisting investigations in domains such as:

- Physics

- Biology

- Chemistry

- Medicine

- Artificial Intelligence

- Robotics

- Materials Science

- Space Exploration

- Climate Science

- Mathematics

without changing its fundamental philosophy.

---

# Guiding Principle

Whenever a new feature is proposed, one question must be asked:

"Does this improve HECE's ability to perform structured scientific investigation?"

If the answer is no, the feature probably does not belong in the project.

---

# Definition of Success

HECE succeeds when users leave with a better understanding of a scientific problem than when they arrived.

Not necessarily with more answers.

But always with better questions.
